Before Salesforce ALM

Reporting in the SRD.

Before the Salesforce QA Application Lifecycle Management system existed, all QA activities lived inside the SRD. Reporting was limited to what could be captured in a spreadsheet — ownership and status counts by tab. There was no traceability, no issue analysis, no pass rates, and no way to drill down into results. The TEAM tab was the only reporting available.
